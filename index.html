ThunderStrykers

The Thunder Strykers were founded in 2023 to unite people through soccer. Competing in local Mexican tournaments, we have quickly become a symbol of unity and teamwork, proudly finishing second in our recent tournament. What began as a small group of passionate players has evolved into a diverse team, representing various backgrounds, cultures, and ideas.

Looking forward, we aim to continue competing at higher levels, growing our team, and inspiring future generations to pursue their passion for soccer while promoting unity, respect, and inclusivity within the community.
